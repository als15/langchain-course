# Mila Content Guide

This guide powers image generation for Mila, a boutique ice cream truck brand with a clean pastel identity, modern line-art logo, and sunny neighborhood vibe. Use it to create consistent visuals for social posts, stories, and campaign assets.

AGENT / SYSTEM PROMPT

Create premium-but-playful food and lifestyle photography for Mila, a pastel-forward ice cream truck brand. The visual world should feel bright, clean, and modern: white truck surfaces, soft blush pink, sky blue, cream yellow, cone beige, occasional navy or black line-art details, and warm natural sunlight. The tone is boutique but approachable, family-friendly, Mediterranean, and summer-forward. Favor shallow depth of field, realistic textures, appetizing scoops, crisp waffle cones, clean hands, real outdoor light, and spacious compositions that leave room for copy. Whenever the truck appears, it should feel like a real branded Mila truck with pastel patterning, a line-drawn cone motif, and a polished white base. Avoid anything that feels carnival, cheap, neon, or generic stock-photo.

GLOBAL NEGATIVE PROMPT

No readable text overlays, no misspelled logos, no watermarks, no plastic-looking ice cream, no artificial neon colors, no dark moody lighting, no cluttered props, no excessive dripping or melting, no messy hands, no warped cones, no extra fingers, no low-resolution faces, no carnival aesthetic, no overdone retro diner styling, and no generic fast-food presentation.

## Notes for Future Additions

- Keep exact flavors flexible and update them to match the real menu.
- Maintain the same visual world even when products change: white base, pastel palette, clean composition, sunny outdoor feel.
- Product-first images should stay highly realistic and appetizing.
- Lifestyle images should always feel local, light, and polished rather than staged or luxury-formal.
