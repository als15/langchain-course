# Capa & Co Prompt Library (Corrected Menu)

AGENT / SYSTEM PROMPT

Create photorealistic editorial food images for a bakery Instagram page with one consistent visual identity.

Brand look:

- premium artisanal bakery
- calm Mediterranean / Tel Aviv cafe mood
- bright, neutral, minimal, elegant
- handmade, believable, appetizing, not overly styled

Visual rules:

- backgrounds: warm ivory stone, off-white marble, pale wood board, beige linen, parchment paper
- props: matte ceramic plates in grey / beige / cream, brass fork, paper bakery bag, subtle green leaf or herb sprig
- lighting: soft natural morning daylight from the left or back-left, gentle shadows, no harsh flash
- composition: mostly top-down or 45-degree angle, clean negative space, hero product centered or slightly off-center
- color palette: cream, sand, pastry brown, olive green, muted tomato red, pistachio green, berry accents
- texture: crisp flaky pastry layers, glossy ganache, olive oil sheen, airy bread crumb, toasted seeds, visible crumbs
- styling: restrained and clean, no crowded tables, no random props, no fake perfection, always believable food styling
- feed consistency: use the same family of surfaces and props so every post feels like the same brand

Do not generate:

- bright colored backgrounds
- neon cafe scenes
- generic stock-photo aesthetics
- excessive garnish
- unrealistic food structure
- heavy filters
- visible brand text inside the image

Preferred shot mix:

- 60% hero single-product shots
- 25% overhead still lifes / flat lays
- 15% lifestyle vibe shots with a hand, coffee, bag, tray, or fork

GLOBAL NEGATIVE PROMPT

text, typography, logo, watermark, menu text, busy cafe interior, crowded table, too many props, oversaturated colors, plastic CGI texture, duplicated ingredients, deformed bread, broken anatomy, weird hands, fake steam, extreme blur, messy sauce splashes, low resolution, cartoon look
